How it works

CAMELTOW replaces phone trees with a fast, mobile-first flow that gets people from panic → paid → released.

1) Scan the QR

The tow operator leaves a QR sticker/placard. Scanning opens your recovery page with the correct yard and case token.

2) Confirm your vehicle

Enter plate + state to match the tow record and display where your car is stored and what fees are required.

3) Choose speed or delivery

Pick standard pickup, fast-track processing, or delivery to you via verified drivers (where available).

4) Pay online

Pay from your phone. Get a digital receipt and release instructions—no cash, no counter surprises.

5) Track status

See confirmation, release progress, and delivery ETA (if you choose delivery). Everything in one place.

6) Get your car back

Less time lost, fewer angry calls for tow yards, and a cleaner process for everyone involved.

FAQ

Common questions from customers and tow partners.

Does CAMELTOW set tow fees?

No. Tow/storage fees are determined by the yard, property owner, or municipality. CAMELTOW adds an optional service layer (payment, fast-track, delivery).

What if I don't see a QR sticker?

In a full rollout, you can still start by searching plate + city. QR is the fastest path because it ties to the correct yard instantly.

Is delivery always available?

Delivery depends on city coverage, yard rules, and eligibility. You'll see availability before paying.
